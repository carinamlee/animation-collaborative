Testimonials

Our students are some of the most passionate animators and designers in the world! Since The Animation Collaborative opened in 2011, hundreds of students have traveled from all over the world to attend workshops. We are proud of all that our students have accomplished. Many have been awarded prestigious animation internships at studios such as Blue Sky Studios, Pixar Animation Studios, and Walt Disney Animation Studios!

AnimC is the only art school I've attended that employs only the top of the line talent! The instructors here are incredible artists and some of the most dedicated instructors I've ever had. Every instructor makes sure their students gain the knowledge they need to get a job in the industry. I owe the animation internships I've gotten to the amazing staff at AnimC! The workshops are affordable, and they offer a diverse range of workshops, and their headquarters are warm and welcoming! I couldn't recommend AnimC enough!

Nicole Ridgwell
Animator at Nickelodeon

Past: Disney Animation Internship 2013, Pixar Animation Internship 2014

My experience at AnimC was a fruitful experience. Prior to attending, I had only been learning one approach to animation. I enjoyed the layered approach Michal Makarewicz taught because it allowed me to have a more connected experience with my animation... It really helped me jump into my shot and put myself into my work. Learning this technique at AnimC really improved my speed and understanding of the curves in the graph editor. More than anything, it showed me another way to animate that made me feel more connected with my work!

Brock Boyts
Animator at Blue Sky Studios
