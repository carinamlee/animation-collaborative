Testimonials

Our students are some of the most passionate animators and designers in the world! Since The Animation Collaborative opened in 2011, hundreds of students have traveled from all over the world to attend workshops. We are proud of all that our students have accomplished. Many have been awarded prestigious animation internships at studios such as Blue Sky Studios, Pixar Animation Studios, and Walt Disney Animation Studios!

Every instructor makes sure their students gain the knowledge they need to get a job in the industry. I owe the animation internships I've gotten to the amazing staff at AnimC! The workshops are affordable, and they offer a diverse range of workshops, and their headquarters are warm and welcoming! I couldn't recommend AnimC enough!
